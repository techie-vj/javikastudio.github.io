Partnerships

Reach users who are
ready to change

Our users open TidyApp because they want to reduce screen time. They see app recommendations immediately after viewing their own usage data — the highest-intent moment possible.

01
Contextual Matching
Recommendations are matched to each user's top app categories. A heavy social media user sees mindfulness apps. A gamer sees strategy or learning alternatives.
02
No Competing Ads
We only feature wellness, productivity, fitness, and education apps. Social media, games, and entertainment are never shown — protecting the user experience and your brand.
03
Native Format Only
Full app name, icon, description, and CTA button. The same design language as the rest of the app — not a banner ad, not an interstitial. Feels like a recommendation, not advertising.
04
Affiliate via Impact.com
Cost-per-acquisition model. You pay per install or trial start, not per impression. We partner via Impact.com for transparent tracking and reliable payouts.
